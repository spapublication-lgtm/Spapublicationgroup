# SPA Publication Group - Website PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Create a professional, responsive website for SPA Publication Group - an academic publishing consortium with 5 companies (3 National, 2 International). The website should convey academic excellence, legal authority, and global credibility with navy blue, gold, white, black color scheme.

## User Personas
- **Researchers/Academicians**: Looking to publish research monographs, scholarly books
- **Authors**: Seeking to publish authored academic books
- **Universities/Institutions**: Looking for academic publishing partnerships
- **Students/Educators**: Seeking textbooks and educational materials
- **Legal Professionals**: Looking for legal commentaries and edited volumes

## Core Requirements (Static)
1. **Brand Identity**: Navy blue, gold, white, black tones - scholarly/academic aesthetic
2. **5 Companies**:
   - SPA Publications (National - Authored/Research Books)
   - Legal Luminaries Publication House (National - Edited Books)
   - Intellect Jurists Publishers (National - Textbooks)
   - Blue Globe International (International)
   - Aquitas International (International)
